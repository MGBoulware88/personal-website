The Beginning

Realizing the immense influence technology has on our world was a paradigm-shifting moment for me. Previously, I had taken for granted the role technology played in our daily lives, from the devices we use to the networks that bind us. However, upon further exploration, I came to comprehend the depth and breadth of technology's impact on society. It has revolutionized how we interact, learn, work, and play, permeating every facet of our existence. As technology progresses at breakneck speed, it is critical to comprehend the societal ramifications of these advancements and reflect on the ethical and social responsibilities that accompany them.

Why I Chose the Tech Industry

Technology has revolutionized the way we approach health and wellness, empowering individuals to take control of their own physical and mental well-being like never before. From cutting-edge health apps to wearable devices, technology has given us the ability to track and monitor our fitness levels, manage our diets, and receive customized health recommendations. With this newfound knowledge, we are better equipped to live healthier, more active lives, and to make informed decisions about our health. Moreover, technology has brought communities together, creating platforms for individuals to connect and participate in group activities and social support networks. As we continue to innovate and push the boundaries of what is possible, it is imperative that we ensure that everyone has access to these powerful tools, promoting inclusivity and equality in our society.

Starting in middle school, I began volunteering at a Gifted Students Academy at UCI, where I discovered my passion for mentoring and teaching. While in high school, I focused on teaching biology and chemistry, but after graduation, I decided to shift my focus to teaching coding, engineering, and robotics. I taught students of all ages, from elementary to high school, for several years, and I found joy in every moment of it. Being able to share my knowledge and passion for these subjects with the younger generation was incredibly fulfilling, and I look forward to continuing to inspire and mentor others in the future.

As a self-taught software engineer working in retail, one might wonder why someone with technical skills would choose to work in a seemingly unrelated field. However, this individual has been able to use their experience in retail to develop valuable customer service and project management skills that are essential to success in any profession. By learning how to effectively communicate with customers and colleagues, this software engineer has gained insight into how to manage time, delegate tasks, and work collaboratively. Even when faced with difficult situations and unpleasant coworkers, they have learned the importance of maintaining a positive attitude and finding ways to work together towards a common goal. These skills are transferable to any job and will undoubtedly serve them well in their future endeavors as a software engineer.

The value of a piece of paper lies not in its physical form, but in the skills and knowledge that were acquired in the process of earning it. The effort, time, and resources invested in completing projects or working with challenging colleagues do not determine the worth of the paper. Instead, it is the lessons learned from these experiences that shape one's abilities and potential as a software engineer. Every experience, whether good or bad, contributes to personal and professional growth, and prepares one for future challenges. It is important to embrace all experiences as they come, for they collectively shape one's journey towards becoming a better software engineer.

The technology sector is experiencing a significant growth surge, and the opportunities are limitless. The forthcoming years are brimming with thrilling breakthroughs and discoveries that will influence the way we interact, communicate and live. It is crucial to be a part of this revolution since the impact of technology on our lives will be substantial and enduring. As a community, we are working towards surpassing the boundaries of what can be accomplished, and aspiring to achieve extraordinary feats.

Envision a world where cutting-edge technologies that were previously depicted in sci-fi films are now an actuality. From holographic keyboards and interactive tabletops to flying vehicles, the technology sector is bringing our most fantastic reveries to fruition. Innovations in machine learning such as autonomous cars, AI-powered personal assistants, and even robotic surgeries are transforming our lives and professions for the better. With every fresh discovery, we are forging a more promising future, and the potentials are genuinely infinite.

The impetus behind these breakthroughs originates from the trailblazers of the technology industry, including Geoff Hinton, Yoshua Bengio, Yann LeCun, and Jurgen Schmidhuber. Despite facing resistance from their contemporaries in the 1990s and 2000s, these pioneers never lost faith in neural networks. They serve as a brilliant model of what can be accomplished with perseverance and a resolute vision.
