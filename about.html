The Beginning

Discovering how much of an impact technology has on society was a transformative experience for me. I used to take for granted the role that technology plays in our lives, from the smartphones we use to the internet that connects us all. However, as I delved deeper into the subject, I started to realize just how much of an impact technology has on our society. From the way we work and communicate to how we entertain ourselves and stay informed, technology has transformed virtually every aspect of our daily lives. Furthermore, as technology continues to advance at a rapid pace, it is increasingly important to understand its impact on society and to consider the ethical and social implications of these advancements.

Why I Chose the Tech Industry

Technology has made it easier for people to access health-related information, which can help them live better and healthier lives. With the rise of health-related mobile applications and wearables, people can now monitor their physical activity, track their calorie intake, and receive personalized health recommendations. These technologies not only encourage people to live healthier lifestyles but also provide them with the tools and resources to do so. It has brought communities together and encouraged society to live better and healthier lives. It has created a platform for people to connect with each other, access health-related information and services, and participate in community activities. As technology continues to advance, it is crucial to ensure that everyone has access to these technologies to promote inclusivity and equity in our society.

I grew up volunteering at a Gifted Students Academy at UCI starting in middle school. After high school graduation and have fallen in love with mentoring/teaching in terms of biology and chemistry, I navigated over to teaching closer to my field of interest - coding, engineering, and robotics. I taught elementary, middle, and high school students for years and loved every minute.

What's a self-taught software engineer doing working retail? Learning customer service and project management skills such as task delegations and time management. I learned to work with those I was displeased working with with a happy smile.

A piece of paper will always be just that, a piece of paper, but its worth is in the skills a person had developed earning such paper. It's not the time, money, or headache that you put into doing projects you thought were tedious, annoying, or a waste of time. It's not the hours you spent working with people you disliked. At the end of the day, it's what you learned that will make you a better software engineer in the future. Which means, it's everything all combined. All the good and the bad, because those aren't the worse days you'll face and you'll have better days to come.

The tech industry is on the rise and the possibilities are endless! The future is filled with exciting new advancements and innovations that will shape the way we live, interact, and connect with one another. Being a part of this revolution is essential, as the impact of technology on our lives will be profound and long-lasting. Together, as a society, we are pushing the limits of what is possible and reaching for the stars.

Imagine a world where the futuristic technologies once only seen in science fiction movies are now a reality. From holographic keyboards and touchscreen tables to flying cars, the tech industry is making our wildest dreams come true. Advances in machine learning, such as self-driving cars, virtual personal assistants, and even robotic surgeries, are changing the way we live and work for the better. With each new breakthrough, we are forging a brighter future and the possibilities are truly endless.

The driving force behind these advancements are the pioneers of the tech industry like Geoff Hinton, Yoshua Bengio, Yann LeCun, and Jurgen Schmidhuber, who never lost faith in neural networks despite opposition from their peers in the 1990s and 2000s. These innovators are a shining example of what can be achieved with determination and a strong vision.
